I recently wrapped up a comprehensive Power BI dashboard focused on analyzing the Business Profit and Loss of a computer parts distributor, comparing four years of performance. The insights generated from mouse, keyboard, and network product sales data have been incredibly valuable for identifying growth areas and cost efficiencies.

This project was a fantastic opportunity to leverage the full Power BI stack:
Power Query (M): Meticulous data cleaning and transformation to ensure accuracy.
DAX: Crafting complex measures for year-over-year comparisons, profitability margins, and intelligent filtering.
Data Modeling: Structuring the data for optimal performance and intuitive slicing and dicing.
Data Storytelling: Translating the metrics into a compelling, actionable narrative for stakeholders.
It's amazing how the right visualization can bridge the gap between abstract numbers and tangible business decisions.
